# ZilMate Swarm Strategy: The "Digital Corporation" (Refined)

To build a "Perfect" end-to-end business swarm, ZilMate must move beyond individual tool execution into **Hierarchical Role-Based Intelligence**.

---

## 1. The 10 "Real-World" Super Tools
These tools provide the "Connective Tissue" between the AI and the global economy. While Composio provides the API transport, ZilMate provides the **Autonomous Logic** for:

1.  **Global Finance Intelligence (Yahoo Finance):** Live ticker quotes, historical data, and macroeconomic trend analysis (via `yahoo-finance2`).
2.  **Web-Scale Intelligence (Firecrawl):** Deep recursive crawling of competitor sites to extract pricing models and feature sets into clean Markdown.
3.  **Multi-Modal Browser Vision (Playwright + Vision):** Not just "clicking" links, but "seeing" the page via Gemini-3.1-Vision to verify UI integrity and user flows.
4.  **Corporate Memory Graph (Redis):** A shared departmental knowledge base where a fact learned by the SEO agent is immediately actionable by the Content Writer.
5.  **Cross-App Financial Ledger:** A meta-tool that correlates Stripe revenue with HubSpot deal stages and GitHub PR costs to calculate real-time ROI.
6.  **Supply Chain Watchdog:** Integrating Shopify inventory with logistics APIs (UPS/FedEx) to proactively notify the user of stockouts or shipping delays.
7.  **Growth Funnel Analytics:** Directly fetching and analyzing Google Analytics 4 and Search Console data to determine the next growth experiment.
8.  **Automated Sales Outreach:** Chaining LinkedIn data extraction with personalized Gmail sequences and HubSpot deal creation.
9.  **Legal & Compliance Auditor:** Automatic scanning of PDF contracts and internal docs for GDPR, SOC2, or specific business policy violations.
10. **Business Intelligence (BI) Engine:** Generating professional PDF/Slide Deck reports that synthesize data from all 20 agents into a single executive briefing.

---

## 2. The 20 Specialist Subagents
We organize the swarm into five core departments to ensure precision and prevent context window saturation.

### A. Strategy & Leadership (The Vision)
1.  **CEO Orchestrator:** Top-level planning and inter-departmental delegation.
2.  **Product Manager:** Translates vision into Linear tickets and feature specs.
3.  **Market Analyst:** Conducts competitor research using Web Intelligence.

### B. Engineering & SRE (The Builders)
4.  **Architect:** Designs technical schemas and implementation plans.
5.  **Full-Stack Coder:** Writes code, handles PRs, and manages the Git repo.
6.  **QA Engineer:** Runs browser automation tests and verifies UX.
7.  **DevOps SRE:** Ensures uptime, monitors logs, and manages CI/CD.

### C. Growth & Marketing (The Amplifiers)
8.  **Growth Hacker:** Runs A/B tests and optimizes the conversion funnel.
9.  **SEO Expert:** Audits site health and performs keyword research.
10. **Content Writer:** Drafts blog posts, newsletters, and social copy.
11. **Social Media Manager:** Manages community engagement across platforms.
12. **Sales Rep (SDR):** Handles outbound prospecting and lead scoring.
13. **Ads Manager:** Optimizes paid campaign spend (Google/Meta).

### D. Operations & Finance (The Foundation)
14. **Finance Analyst:** Monitors MRR, manages payouts, and tracks burn rate.
15. **Customer Success:** Resolves user tickets by indexing internal docs.
16. **Legal Counsel:** Reviews contracts and ensures regulatory compliance.
17. **HR Recruiter:** Sources talent (or new agent skills) for the company.
18. **Logistics Lead:** Coordinates shipping and inventory for physical/digital goods.

### E. Data & Insights (The Truth)
19. **Data Scientist:** Builds SQL reports and predictive business models.
20. **BI Reporter:** Generates the "Weekly Business Summary" for the CEO.
